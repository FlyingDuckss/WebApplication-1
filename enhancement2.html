Enhancements


Time Out

This improvement incorporates a countdown timer into the job application form, offering a visual representation of the time remaining for form completion. It enriches user engagement by providing clear visual signals as the deadline approaches.

To experience this enhancement

To observe this enhancement:

  • To initiate interaction with the form located on the page.Apply for a Job page.
  • Once any form field is clicked, the timer initiates immediately and remains visible throughout.
  • As time elapses, the timer's color shifts from green to red, signaling increasing urgency.
